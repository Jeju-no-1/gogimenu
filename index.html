How to Order Meat
Fresh meat (Hanwoo beef & Jeju pork) is displayed in the butcher case. Pick your cut at the counter — staff will bring it to your table for grilling. Items below are additional dishes you can order.

Signature
Hanwoo Beef Tartare
한우육회
₩25,000
Hand-cut raw Hanwoo beef seasoned with sesame oil, garlic, and topped with egg yolk. A Korean delicacy.
Mix the egg yolk into the beef before eating. Pair with pear slices for sweetness.
Hanwoo Beef Sashimi
한우육사시미
₩25,000
Thinly sliced, lightly seared premium Hanwoo beef. Served chilled with dipping sauce.
Dip in the accompanying wasabi soy sauce. Enjoy the delicate, melt-in-your-mouth texture.

Hanwoo Bone Soup

₩12,000
Rich, milky-white beef bone broth slow-simmered for hours. Served with rice and sliced beef.

Traditional Korean stew made with fermented soybean paste, tofu, and vegetables. Mild and savory.
